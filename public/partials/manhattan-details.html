Manhattan Cafe & Theater

About

Manhattan is one of the most successfull restaurant/night clubs in Recife, which offers an innovative format of Cafe-theater, with good quality music and awarded gastronomy goods bringing people interested on having fun with friends and family.

Role

I was responsible for design and implement the front-end of the website, which counts with responsive approach and html5 page change without refresh the whole screen.

Results

Regarding to performance and mantainability, The technical outcomes are stunning. The separation of concerns (API,front-end, ) were crucial to help developers on future implementations, decreasing the risks failing builds and other errors.

For the users, the new layout and functionalities became more attractive and easy to use, including the responsive features, which didn't existed at former version.
